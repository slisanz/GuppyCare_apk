# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

GuppyCare ("the app") is a companion app for the GuppyCare aquarium device. This policy explains what data the app handles and why.

## Who is responsible

The app is published by the GuppyCare developer. For any question about this policy or your data, contact: slisanz159@gmail.com

## Data the app collects

When you sign in and use the app, the following data is processed through Google Firebase:

- Google account information: your email address, display name, and a user id, obtained when you sign in with Google. This is used only to identify your account and link it to your device.
- Device pairing data: the id of the GuppyCare device you connect to, and whether you are the owner or a shared user.
- Device settings and readings: feeding schedule, feed amount, water sensor (TDS) values, last feed time, and online status. These are stored so the app and the device stay in sync.
- Notification token: a Firebase Cloud Messaging token, used only to send you a notification when the device reports dirty water.

The app does not collect location, contacts, photos, or any data unrelated to running the device. The app contains no advertising and no third party analytics.

## How the data is used

The data is used only to operate the app: to sign you in, to connect your phone to your device, to show and change the device settings, and to send the water quality notification. The data is not sold and is not used for advertising.

## Where the data is stored

Account and device data is stored in Google Firebase (Authentication, Realtime Database, and Cloud Messaging). Google processes this data as the infrastructure provider. See the Google Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy for how Google handles data on its platform.

## Data sharing

A device can be shared with other users that you allow. Those users can see and change that device's settings. Apart from this, your data is not shared with anyone.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data stays in the database while you use the app. To delete your data, sign out and contact slisanz159@gmail.com with the email address you used to sign in, and the associated account and device records will be removed.

## Children

The app is a tool for operating aquarium hardware and is not directed at children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same address with a new date at the top.
